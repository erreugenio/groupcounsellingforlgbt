About Our Program

Compassionate guidance for your authentic self.

Validating Environment

Our counseling program offers an affirming space for individuals navigating their LGBTQ+ identity. Whether you are questioning, coming out, or seeking community, our sessions provide the emotional safety you deserve.

Peer Connection

Connect with others who understand the unique challenges and joys of LGBTQ+ experiences. Share stories, build resilience, and discover the power of a supportive community.

What to Expect

  • Confidential Sessions Weekly 90-minute group meetings in a private setting.
  • Guided Conversations Discussions led by professional, LGBTQ+-affirming counselors.
  • Respect for Identity Absolute respect for all names, pronouns, and identities.

Who Can Join?

This group specifically supports individuals who:

  • Identify as part of the LGBTQ+ community or are currently questioning their identity.
  • Are 18 years of age or older.
  • Can commit to weekly attendance for the 8-week cycle.
  • Agree to uphold strict confidentiality and mutual respect for all members.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your emotional safety is our highest priority. All registrations and sessions are strictly confidential. We pledge to use only your preferred name and pronouns in all communications. Information is never shared outside the counseling office without your explicit consent.
